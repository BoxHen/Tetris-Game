How To Play

Movement: Use the arrow keys to control left, right and, down motion of the tetrominoes. Use the up arrow to rotate (q and w keys will rotate the pieces too)

Scoring: Each consecutive line complete will award more points. One line is worth 100pts, then 300pts for 2 lines, 700pts for 3 lines, and 1500pts for 4 lines (a tetris)

Tatics: Use the Next block to see what the next piece will be. Use this to plan your moves carefully.

Tip: Use the shift key to hold pieces if you got a bad piece or save a piece for big plays
